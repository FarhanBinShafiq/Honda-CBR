Honda CB200X

Honda Motorcycle and Scooter India (HMSI) today launched the new CB200X in the country at Rs 1,44,500 (ex-showroom, Gurugram). While bookings for the Honda CB200X are now open, it will start arriving at the dealerships from next month. The new Honda CB200X employs the same 184cc PGM-Fi engine used by the Honda Hornet 2.0. The engine develops 17bhp of maximum power and 16.1Nm peak torque, and is paired with a five-speed gearbox.
